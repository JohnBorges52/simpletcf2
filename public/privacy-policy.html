Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

SimpleTCF respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with Canadian privacy legislation.

  1. Information We Collect — Personal information (name, email address, account login data, payment transaction references) and technical information (IP address, device type, browser data, usage analytics).
  2. How We Use Information — Provide and operate the platform, process payments, improve learning performance tools, communicate account updates, and detect fraud or unauthorized activity.
  3. Payment Processing — Payments are processed securely by third-party providers such as Stripe. SimpleTCF does NOT store full credit card details.
  4. Firebase Services — SimpleTCF uses Google Firebase for authentication, data storage, hosting, and analytics. Google may process data according to their privacy policy.
  5. Analytics — We use Google Analytics or equivalent tools to understand user behavior and improve services. You may disable analytics through browser privacy settings.
  6. Data Security — We implement industry-standard security safeguards. However, no online system can guarantee absolute security.
  7. Data Retention — We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.
  8. User Rights (Canadian Compliance) — You may request access to your personal data, correction of inaccurate data, or account deletion. Contact: support@simpletcf.com
  9. Cookies — SimpleTCF uses cookies to maintain login sessions, track performance data, and improve user experience. You may disable cookies through browser settings.
